# Gulf Gate brand guidelines

LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED BEFORE PUBLIC LAUNCH — brand assets do not imply licensing.

## Idea

The mark is a **geometric portal** with a dual **G / G** monogram and a cyan flow arrow.
It signals controlled digital-asset request operations — clarity, transition, and trust — without mimicking banks, licensed MSBs, Binance, or Tether.

## Colours

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Deep Navy | `#071426` | Page background, favicon field |
| Navy Surface | `#0C1D33` | Cards / elevated panels |
| Metallic Gold | `#C9A24D` | Primary brand strokes |
| Warm Gold | `#E1BD69` | Highlights / wordmark |
| Cyan Accent | `#28C7D9` | Actions, flow, focus |
| Off White | `#F4F7FA` | Primary text on dark |
| Muted Text | `#94A3B8` | Secondary text |
| Success / Warning / Danger | `#22C55E` / `#F59E0B` / `#EF4444` | Status only |

Gold is **accent**, not wallpaper. Prefer cyan for interactive affordances.

## Typography

- Arabic: IBM Plex Sans Arabic (`next/font`)
- English / UI: Manrope (`next/font`)
- Do not embed unlicensed font files in SVG assets.

## Safe area & minimum size

- Keep **≥ 12%** clear space around the symbol equal to the stroke gap of the gate frame.
- Symbol minimum: **16×16** (favicon), **24×24** in UI, prefer **32×32+**.
- Horizontal lockup minimum width: ~140px.

## Light / dark

- Dark UI: gold mark + warm gold wordmark on navy.
- Light UI: deeper gold / navy wordmark (`logo-light`).

## Incorrect use

- Do not stretch, rotate, or recolour arbitrarily.
- Do not add drop shadows, glow stacks, or 3D bevels.
- Do not place the mark on busy photography without a navy scrim.
- Do not claim licensing, custody, or “guaranteed” execution in lockups.
- Do not replace the monogram with a stock shield/lock/coin.

## Header / Footer / Auth

- Header: compact symbol or dark horizontal mark + AR/EN + auth CTAs.
- Footer: dark mark + legal links only (no invented company data).
- Auth: dark mark above the form; secure-session copy without false “email verified” claims.
